CORGIS Data Lab

This data is from the CORGIS Dataset Project and can be found at https://think.cs.vt.edu/corgis/json/immigration/immigration.html

This data contains statistics about immigration into the United States from countries around the world between 2005-2014. Including data such as

  • Enforcement of Immigration Security
  • Naturalizations
  • Legal Permanent Residences
  • Nonimmigrant Admissions

The source of this data is from the United States Department of Homeland Security and can be found at https://www.dhs.gov/immigration-statistics/yearbook
